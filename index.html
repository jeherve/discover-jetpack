How do I install it?

  1. In your dashboard, Plugins > Add New > Jetpack.
  2. Connect the plugin to your WordPress.com account.

Jetpack is a bridge between WordPress.com and your self-hosted WordPress site.

Why should I install it?

  • It includes all the features you need when starting a new blog.
  • Was your site on WordPress.com before? You'll get all the features you used on WordPress.com.
  • It's modular. You can use one of its features, or all of them.
  • It's easier to update one plugin than 30.
  • Leverage the WordPress.com cloud, thus saving resources on your own server.
  • Free support! 😊
